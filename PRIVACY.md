# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 2026**

## 1. What We Do

Contxt.to ("we", "us", "our") is a context-sharing platform. You paste knowledge, we generate a short link. Anyone who opens that link sees a summary and can continue the conversation in ChatGPT, Gemini, or Claude.

This policy explains what data we collect, why, and what you can do about it.

## 2. What We Collect

### Information You Provide

| Data | When | Why |
|------|------|-----|
| **Content** | When you paste text and create a link | This is the core service — you share knowledge via a link |
| **Email address** | When you claim a link to receive visit notifications | To send you the notification you requested |
| **Page metadata** (title, summary) | Auto-generated by AI from your content | To display a preview when the link is shared |

### Information Collected Automatically

| Data | When | Why |
|------|------|-----|
| **IP address** | When anyone visits a contxt.to page | For basic analytics and abuse prevention |
| **User-Agent** | When anyone visits a contxt.to page | To determine browser vs. AI agent (we serve different layouts) |
| **Referrer** | When anyone visits via a link | To understand how links are shared |
| **Timestamp** | When content is created or a link is visited | For service operation and analytics |
| **Cookie (`next-auth.session-token`)** | Only for logged-in users | Session management (optional — most users never log in) |

### Third-Party Services

We use the following services that may process data:

- **Vercel** — hosting. Logs include IP addresses, request paths, and timestamps per their standard logging.
- **Turso (libSQL)** — database. Stores the content, metadata, and claims you create.
- **Google Fonts** — font delivery. May log your IP address per their policy.
- **OpenAI / Anthropic / Google** — AI providers used to generate title and summary from your content. Only the content you paste is sent; no personal data is included.

## 3. How We Use Your Data

- **To provide the service** — generate links, display content, send notifications if you claim a link.
- **To improve the service** — aggregate analytics (which features are used, not who uses them).
- **To prevent abuse** — rate limiting, spam detection, IP blocking if needed.
- We do **not** sell your data. We do **not** use your content for training AI models.

## 4. Data Retention

| Data | Retention Period |
|------|-----------------|
| Content and metadata | Indefinitely, or until you request deletion |
| Email (claimed links) | Until you request deletion or the related link is deleted |
| IP logs (Vercel) | ~30 days per Vercel's standard retention |
| AI-generated metadata | Same as the content it describes |

You can request deletion of any content at any time. See Section 7.

## 5. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

If you are in the EU or California, you have the right to:

- **Access** — request a copy of all data associated with your content or email
- **Deletion** — request permanent removal of your content and associated data
- **Correction** — fix inaccurate data (e.g., update your email)
- **Portability** — receive your content in a machine-readable format
- **Objection** — object to our processing of your data

## 6. Cookies

We use one cookie: `next-auth.session-token` — only if you log in via Google or GitHub to claim a link. This is a session cookie; it expires when you log out or after a period of inactivity.

We do not use tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or third-party marketing cookies.

## 7. Contact & Requests

To exercise any of your rights or ask questions about this policy:

**Email:** privacy@contxt.to

Please include the claim token from your link (if applicable) so we can locate your data quickly.

## 8. Changes

If we change this policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top. If changes are significant, we'll notify users via email (if you provided one) or post a notice on the site.
