01 — Philosophy

The essence of Contxt

Why this brand exists and what it stands for.

Contxt exists at the intersection of knowledge and conversation. We live in a world where the best insights are scattered across Slack threads, meeting notes, and research docs—and the hardest part of any discussion is getting everyone up to speed. Contxt removes that friction.

A single link carries the full picture. The conversation begins where context ends.

The brand is warm but precise. It welcomes curiosity while rewarding clarity. It borrows the elegance of editorial design—Playfair Display’s italic grace, the quiet structure of DM Sans—and pairs it with a bold, unmistakable signature: pink. Not a shy pink, not a pastel pink. A confident, slightly daring pink that says “this is different.”

Brand archetype: The Creator + The Sage. We help people make and share knowledge. We remove barriers between thinking and discussing.

Tagline: Your context, in a single link.

Use Playfair Display for all headings above 18px. Use DM Sans for all body copy, labels, buttons, and UI text. Use JetBrains Mono for code, URLs, and technical references.
Don’t
Mix more than three typefaces on any surface. Never use Playfair Display for body text below 16px — it loses legibility. Never stretch or distort type.
05 — Voice & Tone

How we speak.

Warm, clear, and human. No jargon, no hype.

✧

Warm, never saccharine

Contxt is friendly but professional. We use contractions. We avoid exclamation marks at the end of every sentence. Warmth comes from clarity, not enthusiasm.

◈

Precise, never cold

We say exactly what we mean in as few words as possible. Precision doesn’t mean dry — it means every word earns its place.

⊹

Human, never corporate

No “leverage,” no “synergize,” no “seamlessly.” Write like a smart person explaining something to another smart person. Direct, honest, specific.

⌘

Confident, never arrogant

We know what we are. We don’t oversell. The product speaks for itself — copy gets out of the way.

✓ Do “Paste anything. Get a link. Your team sees the gist and picks up in any AI tool. No account needed.”
✗ Don’t “Leverage our revolutionary AI-powered context-sharing platform to seamlessly transform your team’s knowledge management workflow!”
✓ Do “We ran 3 design sprints this quarter. Key wins: onboarding redesigned at 87% usability. Key misses: dashboard overhaul needs another iteration.”
✗ Don’t “The product design sprint retrospective for the first quarter of fiscal year 2026 contains actionable insights and key performance indicators...”
